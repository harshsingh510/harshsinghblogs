Who am I ?

First of all, Thanx for being here and visiting our site. Hey Guys this is Harsh Singh, a 2nd year Undergrad at Indian Institute of technology Patna. Never thought that destiny would smash me down next to my hometown, yes I belong from Ara, a small and crowded town in Bihar, quite famous though cause of some catchy songs. Belonging from Ara didn’t affect me at all as my pita ji never decided to settle down so am I. He completed his studies in Maithon, Dhanbad and joined banking sector in Lucknow. When hustle took over all the bhasad then I was planned….5 October 2002. Born and bought up in Lucknow with a tinch of Bihar in it. Lucknow famous for its adab…adab means a way, a way of doing things, famous for its chicken, chicken kebabs & chicken kadai (embroideries) and most importantly for its book market i.e. Aminabad. Being in such a city was a hectic task in itself, imagine surrounded by books…ughh. Though this was not with me. I just loved books, which showed me my career path. A book named The secret of my success... published in 2009 by Nitin Jain IIT-JEE/AIEEE topper comes across me and I don’t know what happens that from the next day IIT was my dream. Motivated nigga heard about IIT, but was not aware about anything else except Kota…. “kota jaoge toh selection pakka hai” …and this line too. Kota the coaching hub of India AKA Coaching Factory, producing hundreds of IITians and thousands of non-iitians Things weren’t difficult as everything was decided for me, after 10th Kota and And then 2 years program then selection then package 1cr…kidding (only that 1cr part). But if the things went in right way how could have been miseries justified with others. JEE 2020 cracked with a rank where not choosing any colleges was good option. Got prepared for the Drop and just smashed the JEE 2021 and landed 25km next to my hometown IIT Patna, though located 35km away in a Industrial Town ‘Bihta’. I guess this would be enough to know about me, you’ll know further as more blogs are released.
